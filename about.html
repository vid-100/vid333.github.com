About Us

Established on 30th August 2018, Drone Academy is located near Nesari Road, Yamekond, Taluka Ajara, District Kolhapur. Our academy is dedicated to building strong, disciplined individuals prepared to serve with pride. With a focus on innovation and excellence, we inspire our cadets to overcome challenges and achieve greatness.

At Drone Academy, our mission is to provide comprehensive military training while instilling values of integrity, courage, and leadership. Each cadet becomes a part of a legacy rooted in patriotism and resilience. Together, we aim to set new standards of excellence in defense training.
